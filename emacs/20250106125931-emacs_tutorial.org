#+title: Emacs Tutorial

~C-n~ → move down (next line)
~C-p~ → move up (prev line)

~C-a~ → beginning of line
~C-e~ → end of line

~C-w~ → cut
~M-w~ → copy
~M-y~ → paste from +clipboard+ kill ring


~C-f~ → move right (forward)
~C-b~ → move left (backward)

~C-x u~ → undo
